Additional Information

Some Important Information & Theory Details

Before your lesson:

  • COVID-19 Safety Precautions: Please wear a mask & use a hand sanitizer
  • Remember your provisional license
  • Get plenty of sleep
  • Avoid Alcohol for 24 hours before lesson
  • Breakfast/Lunch (Get some energy)
  • Double check where and when you're being picked up
  • Choose comfy, non-slip shoes
  • Take glasses if you need them
  • Avoid driving with medical conditions (Check with your GP)

Prepare for your theory:

The theory test is broken up into two parts;

  • Multiple Choice Questions
  • Hazard Perception (Video test to measure your ability to notice hazards)
  • Book your theory test: gov.uk/book-theory-test
  • Once you have passed your theory test you will then be able to book your practical driving test: gov.uk/book-driving-test

We recommended downloading the official DVSA app to revise and get some practice.
